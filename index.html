About ai4dev

Research-driven tooling from UDST.

ai4dev builds open-source developer tools focused on AI code quality, prompt engineering, and LLM-assisted workflows. Our work is based at the University of Doha for Science and Technology, where we research how developers can build more reliable, secure, and efficient software with AI in the loop.
